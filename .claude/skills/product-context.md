# product-context

## Product summary

Antigravity is a single-producer beatstore with two core surfaces:

1. A private dashboard for the producer to manage tracks, projects, playlists, contacts, campaigns, calendar, sales, analytics, store setup, and licensing.
2. A public storefront for buyers to browse, preview, and buy either track licenses or project bundles.

## User model

- Producer: the only authenticated internal operator today.
- Guest visitor / buyer: no account required, email captured at checkout.
- Share recipient: views a tokenized public page tailored by recipient kind.

## Core entities

- Tracks
- Projects
- Playlists
- Licenses
- Contacts
- Campaigns
- Share links
- Purchases
- Creator profile

## Important surfaces

- Dashboard library, projects, playlists, studio, contacts, campaigns, calendar, links, store-editor, sales, analytics, profile, settings, offline.
- Public store catalogue, track detail, producer profile, project bundles, checkout, downloads, and access pages.

## Product truths

- The dashboard is operational and producer-first.
- The storefront is public and conversion-focused.
- Share flows are an important acquisition and collaboration channel.
- Track purchases and bundle purchases are distinct product paths.
